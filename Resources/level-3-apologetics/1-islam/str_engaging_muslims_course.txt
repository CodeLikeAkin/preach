STR U - Engaging Muslims Course
Source: https://training.str.org/index

Stand to Reason University (STR U) is a FREE online training program designed to train Christians as effective ambassadors for Christ.

STR U offers short, interactive, engaging courses on:
- Engaging Muslims
- Engaging Jehovah's Witnesses
- Unbreakable: A Clear Case for Christianity
- Are Science and Faith Compatible?
- Are the Gospels Reliable History?
- Tactics
- Never Read a Bible Verse

Note: The full "Engaging Muslims" course content requires login/sign-up to access. STR U is free but requires account creation.

STR.org is a ministry of Stand to Reason, founded by Greg Koukl, focused on training Christians to think clearly and defend their faith graciously.
