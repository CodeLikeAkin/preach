Exposition of the Trinity for Muslim Readers
Source: https://www.answering-islam.org/Trinity/

A comprehensive page presenting material on the Trinity aimed at Muslim (and non-Muslim) readers.

Recommended starting points:
- Sam Schlorff's "The Trinity and you"
- James Arlandson's "A Brief Explanation of the Trinity"
- John Gilchrist's chapter on "The Doctrine of the Trinity"
- Mr. Anderson's booklet "The Trinity"
- Ernest Hahn's essay "Our Savior God, How Great You Are!"
