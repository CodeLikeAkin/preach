Stand to Reason University (STR U) is a FREE online training program designed to
produce an effective ambassador for Christ. Using short, interactive, and engaging
courses, STR U equips Christians to make an even-handed, yet gracious defense for
Christianity in the public square.

Newest Courses: Engaging Muslims, Unlocking the Mystery of Life, Engaging Jehovah's
Witnesses, Unbreakable: A Clear Case for Christianity

Featured Courses: Are Science and Faith Compatible?, Are the Gospels Reliable
History?, Tactics, Never Read a Bible Verse
