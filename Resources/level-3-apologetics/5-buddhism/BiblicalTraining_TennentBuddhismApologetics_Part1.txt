# Buddhism - Lesson 12

## Explorations in Buddhist Apologetics (Part 1)

This lesson covers five key mudras in Buddhism: renunciation, teaching, meditation, calling the Earth to witness, and prayer. Each gesture holds significant meaning, such as renunciation symbolizing Buddha's rejection of worldly life and the teaching mudra indicating the spread of Dharma. The meditation mudra illustrates deep contemplation, while calling the Earth to witness signifies Buddha's enlightenment. The prayer mudra also parallels some Christian gestures. The lesson also explores the experiences of Buddhist converts to Christianity.
