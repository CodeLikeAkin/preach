Engaging Jehovah's Witnesses - STR University
Source: https://training.str.org/catalog/info/id:159
Instructor: Tim Barnett
Platform: Stand to Reason University (STR U) - FREE (login required)

DESCRIPTION:
In "Engaging Jehovah's Witnesses", Tim Barnett equips Christians with the confidence
and skills to engage in meaningful conversations with Jehovah's Witnesses. Discover
essential techniques - such as how to ask thoughtful questions and recognize logical
fallacies - and learn the importance of patience, preparation, and trusting God with
the outcomes. Tim guides you through biblical insights, practical strategies, and
real-life examples to help you navigate these discussions with both truth and
gentleness. This course goes beyond simply teaching what to say; it shows you how
to think and engage Jehovah's Witnesses in a way that honors Christ.

COURSE MODULES:
I - The Only Question That Matters
II - Jesus Is Jehovah
III - Jesus Is Lord
IV - Answering Objections
V - Being Tactical With JWs

Each module has a questions section.

COMPLETION RULES: Must complete the test "V - Questions"

NOTE: Free course but requires STR U account login/signup to access full content.
