8 of the Best Spurgeon Quotes on the Trinity
November 15, 2018

1. "We are baptized into the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Not into three names, but one name. The Father is God, the Son is God, the Holy Ghost is God; but there are not three Gods, but one God."

2. "The doctrine of the Trinity is a mystery; but it is a revealed mystery, and therefore we believe it. We do not pretend to understand it, but we accept it as the truth of God."

3. "I cannot understand the Trinity; but I can believe it, and I can worship the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost, the one God."

4. "The Father is all God, the Son is all God, the Holy Ghost is all God. There is none greater, none less. They are co-equal, co-eternal, and co-existent."

5. "As there is but one God, so there is but one Mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus; and yet this Mediator is himself God over all, blessed forever."

6. "The Holy Spirit is a person, not a mere influence. He can be grieved, he can be vexed, he can be blasphemed against. He is not a force, but a divine Person."

7. "The Father sent the Son, the Son came, the Spirit applies the work. All three Persons unite in the salvation of a sinner."

8. "We believe in the Father, we believe in the Son, we believe in the Holy Ghost. This is the faith of the church universal. This is the foundation of our hope."
