- **Status:** PASS
- The soul is consistently presented as bearing God's image (Genesis 1:27), being of eternal duration, and having its value demonstrated by the cost of Christ's sacrifice.

- The resurrection is referenced as foundational (Matthew 28:18-20 — the risen Christ gives the Great Commission).
- Christ's exclusivity is affirmed (John 10:9 — "I am the door").

### 1.5 No Moralism as Path to Transformation
- **Status:** PASS
- Transformation is consistently attributed to the work of Christ, the love of God, and the Spirit's work — never to human effort or self-improvement.
