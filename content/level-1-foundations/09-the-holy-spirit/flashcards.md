# The Holy Spirit — Flashcards

## Question 1
**Is the Holy Spirit a person or an impersonal force?**

The Holy Spirit is a Person — the third Person of the triune God. He has intellect, emotion, and will. He teaches, speaks, guides, intercedes, and can be grieved, which no impersonal force can do.

---

## Question 2
**How did the Holy Spirit's work in the Old Testament differ from His work in the New Testament?**

In the Old Testament, the Spirit came upon specific individuals temporarily for specific tasks (judges, kings, prophets). In the New Testament, the Spirit permanently indwells every believer from the moment of salvation.

---

## Question 3
**What does it mean that the Holy Spirit seals believers?**

The sealing of the Spirit means God places His mark of ownership on every believer and gives the Spirit as a guarantee — a down payment — that He will complete the work of redemption He started.

---

## Question 4
**What is the baptism of the Holy Spirit?**

The baptism of the Holy Spirit is an experience distinct from salvation in which a believer is filled with the Holy Spirit for power in witness and ministry. It is first seen in Acts 2 on the day of Pentecost.

---

## Question 5
**What is the purpose of spiritual gifts?**

Spiritual gifts are supernatural enablements given by the Holy Spirit to every believer for the common good. Their purpose is to build up the church, not to make the individual appear spiritual.

---

## Question 6
**What are the gifts of the Spirit listed in 1 Corinthians 12?**

The nine gifts are: word of wisdom, word of knowledge, faith, gifts of healings, working of miracles, prophecy, discerning of spirits, various kinds of tongues, and interpretation of tongues.

---

## Question 7
**What is the fruit of the Spirit, and how is it produced?**

The fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. It is produced by the Holy Spirit in the believer's life as they walk by the Spirit, not by human effort.

---

## Question 8
**What does it mean to walk by the Spirit?**

Walking by the Spirit means ordering your daily life in dependence on and obedience to the Holy Spirit's leading. When you walk by the Spirit, you will not carry out the desires of the flesh.

---

## Question 9
**What does it mean to grieve or quench the Spirit?**

Grieving the Spirit is resisting the Spirit through sin and disobedience (Ephesians 4:30). Quenching the Spirit is suppressing or stifling the Spirit's work and gifts (1 Thessalonians 5:19).

---

## Question 10
**What role does the Holy Spirit play in prayer?**

The Holy Spirit helps believers in their weakness, interceding with groanings too deep for words when they do not know how to pray. He prays through them according to the will of God.
