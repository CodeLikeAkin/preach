# Flashcards: The Trinity

**Level:** Level 1 — Foundations
**Module:** 02 — The Trinity

---

## Flashcard Q&A

### Card 1
**Q:** What is the Trinity?
**A:** The one true God exists eternally as three distinct persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — who share the same divine nature. They are not three gods and not one person wearing masks.

### Card 2
**Q:** Does the Bible teach that God is one or three?
**A:** Both. Deuteronomy 6:4 declares God is one, and passages like Matthew 28:19 and Mark 1:10-11 show three distinct persons. These truths must be held together with equal conviction.

### Card 3
**Q:** What does the Hebrew word "echad" tell us about the oneness of God?
**A:** *Echad* is the same word used in Genesis 2:24 — "they shall become one flesh." It often denotes a compound unity, not a simple, solitary oneness.

### Card 4
**Q:** Is Jesus Christ fully God?
**A:** Yes. John 1:1 says the Word was God, Thomas called Him "my Lord and my God" (John 20:28), and Colossians 2:9 says all the fullness of the Godhead dwells in Him bodily.

### Card 5
**Q:** Why does the deity of Christ matter for salvation?
**A:** Only God could bear the weight of God's wrath against sin. A mere man, no matter how good, could not save us. Jesus had to be both fully God (to save) and fully man (to represent us).

### Card 6
**Q:** Is the Holy Spirit a person or a force?
**A:** The Holy Spirit is a divine Person. He has a mind (Romans 8:27), a will (I Corinthians 12:11), can be grieved (Ephesians 4:30), and lying to Him is lying to God (Acts 5:3-4).

### Card 7
**Q:** What did the Father do in salvation?
**A:** The Father designed salvation before the foundation of the world. He chose us in Christ (Ephesians 1:4), draws people to the Son (John 6:44), and adopts believers as His children (Romans 8:15).

### Card 8
**Q:** What does the Holy Spirit do in the life of a believer?
**A:** He regenerates, indwells, teaches, guides, convicts of sin, bears witness of our adoption, seals us for eternity, and glorifies Jesus. He is God living inside every believer.

### Card 9
**Q:** Why are most illustrations of the Trinity inadequate?
**A:** Illustrations like water (liquid/ice/steam) suggest modalism — one person appearing in three forms. Better to affirm the truth without an illustration than to use one that is misleading.

### Card 10
**Q:** How should the Trinity shape the way we live?
**A:** It shapes prayer (to the Father through the Son by the Spirit), relationships (equality with different roles), worship (Spirit reveals Christ who leads to the Father), and assurance (all three Persons are committed to our salvation).
