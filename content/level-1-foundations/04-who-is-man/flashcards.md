# Flashcards: Who Is Man?
## 10 Q&A Pairs + Memory Verse Drill

---

### Q1 — What does it mean that man is made in the image of God?

**A:** It means humans reflect God in ways nothing else does — mentally (reason, choice), morally (conscience, accountability), and socially (relationship). This is the basis of all human dignity and the imago Dei.

**Passages:** Genesis 1:26-27; Genesis 9:6; James 3:9

---

### Q2 — What is federal headship as it relates to Adam?

**A:** Adam acted as the representative of all humanity before God. When he sinned, all humanity sinned in him and fell with him. His guilt and corruption are imputed to all his descendants.

**Passages:** Romans 5:12-19; 1 Corinthians 15:21-22

---

### Q3 — What were the results of the fall?

**A:** Spiritual death (separation from God), physical death (the body decays), and eternal death (hell). The image of God was marred, creation was cursed, and all mankind became children of wrath.

**Passages:** Genesis 3:14-19; Ephesians 2:1-3; Romans 6:23

---

### Q4 — What is total depravity?

**A:** The doctrine that sin has affected every part of man — mind, will, emotions, and body. Man is not as evil as possible, but no part of his being is untouched by sin. He is spiritually dead and hostile to God.

**Passages:** Romans 3:10-18; Ephesians 2:1; Jeremiah 17:9; Romans 8:7-8

---

### Q5 — What is the sin nature?

**A:** The inherent inclination toward rebellion against God inherited from Adam. Every person is born with it. It is universal, pervasive, and manifests itself in actual sins.

**Passages:** Psalm 51:5; Psalm 58:3; Romans 5:12; Romans 7:14-25

---

### Q6 — What does the Bible mean when it says man is "dead in trespasses and sins"?

**A:** It means man is spiritually dead — unable to respond to God, unable to seek Him, unable to please Him. This is not physical death but spiritual separation from God and inability to come to Him.

**Passages:** Ephesians 2:1-3; Romans 8:7-8; 1 Corinthians 2:14

---

### Q7 — Is the will of fallen man free?

**A:** No. Man's will is in bondage to his nature. He freely chooses what he desires, but his nature is corrupted. He cannot choose God unless God first changes his heart. Salvation is monergistic — a work of God alone.

**Passages:** John 6:44; Romans 3:11; John 8:34; Philippians 2:13

---

### Q8 — What is the difference between soul and spirit?

**A:** The soul (mind, emotion, will) is the seat of personality. The spirit is the deepest part of man that communes with God and has eternal consciousness. Both are immaterial, but the spirit specifically relates to God.

**Passages:** Hebrews 4:12; 1 Thessalonians 5:23; Ecclesiastes 12:7

---

### Q9 — What is regeneration?

**A:** The supernatural work of the Holy Spirit that makes a spiritually dead person alive. It produces repentance and faith. It is not caused by human decision but is the sovereign work of God giving a new nature.

**Passages:** John 3:3-7; Ezekiel 36:26-27; Titus 3:5; 2 Corinthians 5:17

---

### Q10 — What is the hope for fallen humanity?

**A:** In Christ, we become new creations. The image of God is being restored. We are being conformed to the image of Christ. The Spirit empowers us to overcome the old nature. One day we will bear Christ's image perfectly.

**Passages:** 2 Corinthians 5:17; Romans 8:29; Philippians 1:6; 1 John 3:2

---

## Memory Verse Drill

**Prompt:** "God created man in His own image —"
**Response:** " — in the image of God He created him; male and female He created them." (Genesis 1:27)

**Prompt:** "For all have sinned —"
**Response:** " — and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23)

**Prompt:** "The heart is deceitful —"
**Response:** " — above all things, and desperately sick; who can understand it?" (Jeremiah 17:9)

**Prompt:** "Sin entered the world through one man —"
**Response:** " — and death through sin, and in this way death came to all people, because all sinned." (Romans 5:12)

**Prompt:** "And you were dead —"
**Response:** " — in the trespasses and sins." (Ephesians 2:1)

**Prompt:** "Therefore, if anyone is in Christ —"
**Response:** " — he is a new creation; the old has passed away; behold, the new has come." (2 Corinthians 5:17)

**Prompt:** "For by grace you have been saved —"
**Response:** " — through faith. And this is not your own doing; it is the gift of God." (Ephesians 2:8-9)

**Prompt:** "The wages of sin is death —"
**Response:** " — but the free gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord." (Romans 6:23)
