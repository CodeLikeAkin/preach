# Heaven, Hell & Eternity — Flashcards

## Question 1
**What happens immediately after a believer dies?**

A believer who dies is immediately in the presence of the Lord. Paul wrote that to be absent from the body is to be present with the Lord. This is called the intermediate state.

---

## Question 2
**What is the difference between the first resurrection and the second resurrection?**

The first resurrection is spiritual — being made alive in Christ through the new birth. The second resurrection is physical — the bodily resurrection of all people at the end of the age, believers to eternal life and unbelievers to judgment.

---

## Question 3
**What kind of body will believers receive at the resurrection?**

Believers will receive glorified bodies — imperishable, immortal, and transformed, like Christ's resurrection body. What is sown perishable is raised imperishable.

---

## Question 4
**What is hell, and why does it exist?**

Hell is a real place of eternal conscious punishment. It exists because God is perfectly just and holy and cannot ignore sin. Those who reject Christ's payment for sin must bear the punishment themselves.

---

## Question 5
**What is heaven like according to the Bible?**

Heaven is the dwelling place of God, a city of unimaginable beauty where there is no more death, mourning, crying, or pain. The primary glory of heaven is the direct presence of God, and believers will reign with Christ there.

---

## Question 6
**What is the difference between the Judgment Seat of Christ and the Great White Throne Judgment?**

The Judgment Seat of Christ is for believers — it determines reward, not salvation. The Great White Throne Judgment is for unbelievers — it determines their eternal destiny based on their rejection of Christ.

---

## Question 7
**What is eternal life according to the Bible?**

Eternal life is knowing God and Jesus Christ. It is not just endless existence but a relationship with God. It is a present possession for every believer, received at the moment of faith.

---

## Question 8
**Why does hell exist if God is love?**

Hell exists because God is also perfectly just. A just God cannot ignore sin. God's love provided a way of escape through Christ, but those who reject that provision must face the just consequences of their sin.

---

## Question 9
**What are the two motivations for living in light of eternity?**

The two motivations are the fear of judgment (knowing we will give an account to Christ) and the love of Christ (being controlled by His love displayed on the cross). The love of Christ is the greater motivation.

---

## Question 10
**Can a person be saved after death?**

No. The Bible teaches that after death comes judgment (Hebrews 9:27). The rich man and Lazarus illustrate that the chasm between heaven and hell is fixed after death. The time to respond to God is in this life.
