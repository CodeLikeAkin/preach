# Flashcards: Why Souls Matter

**Level:** Level 2 — Evangelism
**Module:** 02 — Why Souls Matter

---

## Flashcard Q&A

### Card 1
**Q:** According to Matthew 16:26, what profit is there in gaining the whole world if you lose your soul?
**A:** There is no profit at all. The soul is of far greater value than anything the world can offer, and nothing can be exchanged for it once it is lost.

### Card 2
**Q:** Why does the reality of judgment make evangelism urgent rather than optional?
**A:** Because every person will face God in judgment, and the only way to be saved from the penalty of sin is through faith in Jesus Christ. If the danger is real, silence is cruelty.

### Card 3
**Q:** What does the cross reveal about both the seriousness of sin and the value of a soul?
**A:** The cross reveals that sin is so serious it required the death of the Son of God, and the soul is so valuable that God was willing to pay that price to redeem it.

### Card 4
**Q:** How is God portrayed in the Bible in relation to lost people?
**A:** God is portrayed as the seeking God. From the garden of Eden ("Where are you?") to the cross, God actively pursues lost sinners rather than waiting for them to find Him.

### Card 5
**Q:** What did Jesus mean when He said to "strive" to enter through the narrow gate?
**A:** Strive means to wrestle or exert intense effort. Jesus was teaching that entering the kingdom requires an active, determined response to God's call — not passive acknowledgment or casual interest.

### Card 6
**Q:** Why is the door to salvation described as narrow?
**A:** The door is narrow because there is only one way in — through Jesus Christ. No amount of good behavior, religion, or sincerity can save a person apart from faith in Christ and His finished work on the cross.

### Card 7
**Q:** What is the difference between knowing you should evangelize and feeling a holy compulsion to do so?
**A:** Duty is knowing you should share the gospel; compulsion is feeling that you must. Compulsion comes from seeing the gospel clearly and being gripped by the love of Christ, not from trying harder to fulfill an obligation.

### Card 8
**Q:** According to 2 Corinthians 5:14, what compels believers to share the gospel?
**A:** The love of Christ compels us. When we truly understand that Christ died for all, we are driven by His love rather than by guilt, pressure, or religious duty.

### Card 9
**Q:** Why is investing in the souls of people the only investment that lasts forever?
**A:** Because everything in this world is passing away — possessions, achievements, reputation — but the human soul is eternal. Only what is done for Christ and His kingdom will endure beyond this life.

### Card 10
**Q:** What promise does Jesus give to those who obey the Great Commission?
**A:** Jesus promised, "Lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age." Believers are not sent alone — Christ's presence accompanies them in every evangelistic effort.
