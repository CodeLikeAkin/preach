# Flashcards — Evangelism in Everyday Life

## 10 Question & Answer Pairs

### Card 1
**Q: What is the difference between event-based evangelism and lifestyle evangelism?**
**A:** Event-based evangelism treats witness as something you do at scheduled times or programs, while lifestyle evangelism integrates witness into your everyday routines, relationships, and conversations as a natural way of living.

### Card 2
**Q: Why does your conduct matter before you ever speak a word about the gospel?**
**A:** Unbelievers watch your life before they will listen to your words. A consistent, humble, kind life gives credibility to your message, while hypocrisy destroys it before you even begin to speak.

### Card 3
**Q: What is the most important thing to do when witnessing to family members?**
**A:** Pray for them daily, love them through actions, serve them without preaching at them constantly, and wait for the Spirit to open a door. Let your changed life be your primary witness.

### Card 4
**Q: How can a believer practice evangelism in the workplace?**
**A:** By working with integrity and diligence as unto the Lord, treating colleagues with respect, looking for natural bridges to spiritual conversations, and being ready to give a gentle answer when someone asks about your hope.

### Card 5
**Q: Why is hospitality an effective evangelism tool?**
**A:** Hospitality builds trust and authenticity. When a person is welcomed into your home and sees your real life, they are more likely to receive your words about Christ as genuine. A shared meal lowers walls that arguments cannot breach.

### Card 6
**Q: What is the most effective way to turn an ordinary conversation toward spiritual things?**
**A:** Ask questions. Questions engage the heart and reduce defensiveness. Simple questions like "What do you think happens after death?" or "Where do you find hope?" can open doors that direct declarations cannot.

### Card 7
**Q: How should you define success in everyday evangelism?**
**A:** Success is obedience, not the number of conversions. If you speak the gospel faithfully, love an unbeliever, and serve them, you have succeeded. The harvest is God's responsibility, not yours.

### Card 8
**Q: What role does prayer play in lifestyle evangelism?**
**A:** Prayer is essential. It prepares your heart, softens the hearts of those you are praying for, opens divine appointments, and reminds you that only God can save a soul. You cannot manufacture opportunities or change hearts through your own efforts.

### Card 9
**Q: What should you do when you wrong an unbeliever?**
**A:** Apologize promptly and sincerely. A genuine apology demonstrates humility and the reality of transformation. It removes a barrier to the gospel and restores the credibility of your witness.

### Card 10
**Q: How can you stay motivated when you see no results from your evangelism efforts?**
**A:** Fix your eyes on the cross. Gratitude for Christ's sacrifice for you is the fuel that sustains you through rejection and discouragement. Stay connected to other believers for encouragement, and remember that God's Word never returns void.
